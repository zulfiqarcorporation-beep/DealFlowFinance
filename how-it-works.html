✦ Funded within 48 hours

Simple financing, fast funding.

You handle the sale. We handle the financing. From application to funding in as little as 48 hours.

1

Merchant submits the application

You fill in your customer's details directly through your DealFlow merchant portal — name, contact info, the amount needed, and what it's for. No paperwork, no back and forth. Takes under two minutes.

2

We review and respond within 24 hours

Our team reviews the application and gets back to you within 24 hours with a decision. If approved, we'll send over a tailored payment plan — clear monthly amounts, terms, and conditions for your customer to review.

3

Customer agrees and signs

Once your customer reviews the payment plan and is happy with the terms, they sign the agreement. Simple, transparent, no surprises.

4

You receive funding within 24 hours

As soon as the docs are signed, DealFlow forwards the full funding amount to you — typically within 24 hours. We take it from there and manage all customer payments going forward.
