So how does DealFlow make money?

We work with a network of exclusive lending partners who fund your customers' purchases. When a deal closes, our lender partners pay us a commission — a standard industry arrangement that keeps the entire experience completely free for merchants and customers alike.

It's a model that works for everyone. You close more sales. Your customers get the financing they need. Our lenders grow their portfolio. And DealFlow earns a commission for making it all happen.
